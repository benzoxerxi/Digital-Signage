Why this platform

Built for real‑world digital signage fleets.

Everything is tuned for screens that must “just work”: offline‑first playback, simple onboarding, and clear multi‑tenant billing.

Reliable playback

Offline‑first Android player

Video files are cached on each device so content keeps playing even when the internet drops. When you change playlists, only deltas are downloaded.

Works with cheap Android TV sticks and boxes – no custom hardware required.
Always on

Watchdog autostart & crash recovery

A tiny companion app starts your player on boot, restarts it if Android kills it, and can pin the screen in kiosk mode to keep staff from exiting playback.

Designed for unattended screens in shops, lobbies, and public spaces.
SaaS ready

Multi‑tenant billing & admin

Each customer gets isolated data, connection codes, and device lists. Track plans, trial vs. paid status, and revenue from a single admin panel.

Stripe‑ready config so you can charge for displays instead of projects.
